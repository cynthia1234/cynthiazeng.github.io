Thank you for taking the interest to learn more about me!


I grew up in Beijing, and went to London for my undergraduate studies.
After completing my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, I worked in the financial industry in London.
I had learned a lot working as a data scientist for a quantitative fund; however, I felt pursuing academic endeavours was a path closer to my heart.
After 1 year in industry, I decided to take a gap year and then return to school.
During the gap year, I spent some time traveling, completed yoga teacher training, and did an internship in Shanghai to stay closer to my parents.

Background:

Interests:

Volunteering:

In the future, I hope to continue my volunteering endeavours, and contribute to making our world a better place.
